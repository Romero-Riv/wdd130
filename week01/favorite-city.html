My favorite city is Monterrey

Surrounded by impressive mountains like Cerro de la Silla , this city combines natural beauty with urban development. It has a vibrant culture, delicious northern Mexican food, and a hardworking spirit that represents the people of the region.

Monterrey is also famous for its entrepreneurial spirit and modern infrastructure, making it one of the most developed cities in the country. Despite its fast-paced growth, it still maintains a strong sense of local identity and pride that makes it special to the people who live there.

    Typical food from this city:
  • Cabrito
  • Tacos de Trompo
  • Machaca con huevo
  • Glorias
